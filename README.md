# Sustainability in Computer Science – Seminar Project


## General Requirements
- Length: 7–15 pages (main text only)  
- Language: English (consistent British or American)  
- Minimum references: 20 academic sources  
- Templates:
  - Paper: Overleaf template  
- Submission format: PDF  

## Paper Structure

### Part 1: Research Questions (at least 4 pages)
- Define 1–2 research questions  
- Questions must relate to the chosen topic  
- Answer them scientifically  

### Part 2: Sustainability in Computer Science (at least 2 pages)
- Explore sustainability topics beyond the presentation  
- Discuss:
  - Additional relevant themes  
  - Importance of these topics  
  - Progress and achievements in the field  
  - Organisations or programs promoting sustainability  
  - Impact of computer science compared to other industries  

### Part 3: Reflection (at least 1 page)
- Reflect on insights gained from:
  - Presentations  
  - Literature research  
  - ChatGPT  
- Explain how your perspective on sustainability has changed  
- Consider impact on future academic or professional decisions  

Non-compliance with guidelines may result in point deductions.

## Citation Requirements
- Every claim must be supported by literature  
- Use proper academic citation style
